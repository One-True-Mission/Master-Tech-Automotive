Master Tech Automotive is Kyle's longest-running independent auto repair shop — family-owned and operated by Duke Smith since 1997. With two ASE Master Certified technicians on staff, an A+ BBB rating, and nearly three decades of community trust, we bring dealership-level expertise to every repair without the dealership price tag.

We take customer service, honesty, and integrity seriously. Every repair comes with a 12-month / 12,000-mile parts and labor warranty, and most extended warranties are accepted. From routine oil changes to major engine overhauls, we do the work right and we stand behind it.

To make your experience as stress-free as possible, we offer free coffee and WiFi in our clean waiting area, free local rides while your car is in the shop, and vehicle pick-up and delivery. Financing is also available with easy approval.

What We Do

Full-Service Repair.
All Makes & Models.

🔄
Transmission

Automatic and standard transmissions, all components including clutches, axles, transfer cases, four-wheel drive, and front-wheel drive systems. Complete drivetrain repair.

⚙️
Engine Repair

Engine tuning, major repairs and replacement, fuel injection cleaning and repair, timing belts, fan belts, and complete engine diagnostics using modern equipment.

🔍
Diagnostics

Electronic computer diagnostics, engine control systems, and complete fault code analysis. We find the real problem accurately — no guesswork, no wasted money.

🛑
Brakes & Undercar

Anti-lock brakes, complete brake system service, chassis inspection, and exhaust repair. Safety work backed by our 12-month / 12,000-mile warranty.

❄️
Heating & A/C

Air conditioning repair and recharge, water pump replacement, radiator service, heater repair, and belt replacement. Stay comfortable year-round in the Texas climate.

⚡
Electrical Systems

Batteries, starters, alternators, ignition diagnosis and repair. Complete electrical system troubleshooting with the tools and experience to fix it right the first time.

🚗
Steering & Suspension

Wheel alignments, shocks and struts, CV boots and axles. We restore handling, ride quality, and tire life on all makes and models, domestic and import.

🔧
Oil Changes & Maintenance

Oil, lube and filter services, tune-ups, emissions, filters, hoses, wipers, and factory maintenance schedules. Keep your vehicle running strong between major repairs.

🔎
Pre-Purchase Inspections

Pre-purchase and pre-trip vehicle checks so you know exactly what you're buying or driving. Honest, thorough inspections from technicians with no financial stake in the outcome.
